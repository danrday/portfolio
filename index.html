About

I'm a multi-disciplinary problem solver with a professional background in film and video editing. Since April 2016 I have been learning software development at Nashville Software School, an immersive learning, 6-month programming bootcamp that teaches current programming fundamentals and best practices. The first 3 months have focused primarily on Javascript, and the latter half on Node.js as a runtime environment for developing server-side web applications. Since starting my programming eduacation, I've become especially interested in data visualization using D3.

I worked as an film editor and menu designer for Oscilloscope Laboratories in NYC before moving to Nashville as a freelancer. I have been recognized by Adobe for web design and the Cannes Film Festival for my short film.

I'm currently seeking a job as a developer in a creative, team-oriented environment with a positive mission statement.
